About Me

Becky graduated from the University of Texas at Dallas in May 2018 with a B.S. in Accounting and Finance. While she enjoyed her studies in business, she was always curious about the world of technology.

Right out of college, Becky started her first job at Frank Recruitment Group. It was there that she had the opportunity to learn more about what exciting career opportunities spanned across the technology field. After hearing other professionals talk about their passions within this field, she decided that she wanted to pursue a career as a software developer.

Becky currently works as a Data Processor at Trintech. Outside of time spent coding, she enjoys cooking, dancing, traveling, and hanging out with friends.
